About Our School

A Legacy of Excellence
Since 1952

66no. Radhakuchi J.B. School is a Government-run Lower Primary School located in Radhakuchi Village, Kamrup District. Established under the Assam Elementary Education Act, we have been a cornerstone of primary education in this region for over seven decades.

The school actively implements government flagship programs including PM POSHAN, NIPUN Bharat, and NEP 2020 guidelines through Samagra Shiksha Abhiyan.
